Queo- Chapter 2: Diosis

"Carpe diem, quam minimum credula postero."

- Horace, Odes

1There is a hard point that all activity of sentient beings turn towards. This is one tentacle of it, the whole thing impossible for any one person or species to grasp. The many different species each have a special talent that is unique to them to further understand it.
