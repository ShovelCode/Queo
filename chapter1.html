Queo - Chapter 1: The Harvest Ripens

"Forsan et haec olim meminisse iuvabit."

- Virgil, Aeneid

1That which must came to pass. The rain came and trees and grass grew. What exists now is the process that began all those periods ago. What exists now is the current state of that initial state, the current state was not predicted but came necessarily. The future is not yet understood. Something not predicated may come to pass. 2The heat beat down on us, the workers of air conditioning moved to return good air.
